FAQ

Who can attend? I'm not much of a programmer, should I still come?
Any adult student currently enrolled in an university may join. If you're new to programming, a hackathon is the best place for you to go to. We'll have mentors on-site ready to assist you with whatever problem you face.
How much does it cost? How large can teams be?
Tickets are completly free, thanks to our generous sponsors. You may team up with up to three other people, to be elligible for prizes. Don't worry if you haven't got a team, we'll help you form one at the event.
